Interview with CiCi Yang

Prelude

Foe this interview, I chose Cici Yang as my partner. Instead of having face to face,
we did it on the “WeChat”, It's a social soft work from China. We know each other for 2 years.
Both we are in Graphic Design major. This class is her first time to touch code and web design.
It's bright new for her, but She think is difficult for her.

Conversation

Q: What is "good" screen-based design?

A: A clear page that can make people focus on what the designer wants the audience to see.
  And also interesting and attractive especially when the content is more academically.

Q: How is 90-making online different then art-making offline?

A: Making art online is different than offline is the hierarchy. Which when making art offline,
  people can ignore the hierarchy a little than focusing on the contents. Art making online needs
  more attention for hierarchy is because a messy page will distract the audience and will lose interests.

Q: What do you think is the ‘purpose’ of the web?

A: Depends on what information you want to present.

Q: How has your view of the internet?

A: During the old days, internet did not have much information than nowadays, you can easily
  collect infos you want to know by just sitting in front of your computer or you phone.

Q: How does the web impact graphic design?

A: As I mentioned earlier, when internet did not have a system of running it, graphic design was
  only drawing on paper than can work online now.

Q: What will screen based design grow in the future?

A: It depends on how much more possibilities of internet grows, that there can happens more
  and more different ways of designing.

Q: What do you think internet will grow in future?

A: I hope in the future, that internet can become something that needy for everyone either
  the person has the ability to access it or does not able to access it. It can be fair situation
  for everybody no matter the social status.
